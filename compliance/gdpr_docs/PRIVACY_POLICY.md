## 4. How We Use Your Data

### 4.1 Purpose
We process personal data for the following purposes:

1. **Compliance with EU/Schengen 90/180-Day Rule**
   - Track employee travel days within EU/Schengen area
   - Calculate remaining days available in rolling 180-day window
   - Generate compliance reports

2. **Administrative Purposes**
   - Manage employee travel records
   - Generate export reports (CSV, PDF)
   - Audit trail for data access

### 4.2 Lawful Basis
We process personal data under the following lawful bases:

- **Legal Obligation:** Compliance with Schengen Area travel regulations (90/180-day rule)
- **Legitimate Interest:** HR management and travel compliance monitoring

---

## 5. Data Storage

### 5.1 Location
- **Storage Location:** Local filesystem (EU-based server)
- **Database:** SQLite database file
- **Backups:** Encrypted backups stored locally

### 5.2 Security Measures
- Data encryption at rest (AES-256-GCM)
- Secure session management (HttpOnly, SameSite=Strict cookies)
- Access control (admin-only access)
- Audit logging (immutable audit trail)
- Daily integrity checks (SHA-256)

### 5.3 Retention Period
- **Default Retention:** 36 months after trip exit date
- **Configurable:** Retention period can be adjusted via admin settings
- **After Retention:** Data is automatically purged and permanently deleted

## 6. Your Rights Under GDPR

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

### 6.1 Right of Access (Article 15)
You can request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.

**How to Exercise:** Contact your HR department or use the "Export" function in the Privacy Tools section (admin access required).

### 6.2 Right to Rectification (Article 16)
You can request correction of inaccurate personal data.

**How to Exercise:** Contact your HR department or use the "Rectify Name" function in the Privacy Tools section (admin access required).

### 6.3 Right to Erasure (Article 17)
You can request deletion of your personal data.

**How to Exercise:** Contact your HR department or use the "Delete Employee" function in the Privacy Tools section (admin access required).

**Note:** Deletion will remove all trip records associated with the employee.

### 6.4 Right to Restrict Processing (Article 18)
You can request restriction of data processing.

**How to Exercise:** Contact your HR department or Data Protection Officer.

### 6.5 Right to Data Portability (Article 20)
You can request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

**How to Exercise:** Use the "Export" function to download your data as JSON/CSV.

### 6.6 Right to Object (Article 21)
You can object to processing based on legitimate interests.

**How to Exercise:** Contact your HR department or Data Protection Officer.

### 6.7 Rights Related to Automated Decision-Making
We do not use automated decision-making or profiling.
