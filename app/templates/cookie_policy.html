Cookie Policy

How we use cookies in ComplyEur

Last Updated: {{ current_date }}
This Cookie Policy explains how ComplyEur uses cookies and similar technologies.

1. Overview

ComplyEur is designed with privacy in mind. We use only essential cookies that are necessary for the secure operation of the application. We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or any third-party analytics cookies.

2. What Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are strictly necessary for the application to function securely:

Session Cookie

  • Purpose: Maintains your login session securely
  • Duration: Session-based (expires when you close your browser or after inactivity)
  • Type: HTTP-only, Secure (HTTPS only), SameSite=Lax
  • Contains: Session identifier only (no personal data)

Cookie Consent Cookie

  • Purpose: Remembers your cookie consent preference
  • Duration: 90 days
  • Type: Secure (HTTPS only), SameSite=Strict
  • Contains: Simple consent flag ("true")

3. What We Don't Use

ComplyEur does not use:

  • Tracking cookies
  • Advertising cookies
  • Analytics cookies (Google Analytics, etc.)
  • Third-party cookies
  • Social media cookies
  • Marketing cookies

We do not share cookie data with any third parties, and we do not use cookies to build profiles of your browsing behavior.

4. Managing Cookies

Since we only use essential cookies, you cannot disable them without affecting the functionality of the application. However, you can:

  • Clear your browser cookies to remove stored consent preferences
  • Use your browser's privacy settings to block cookies (note: this will prevent you from logging in)
  • Contact your system administrator if you have questions about cookie usage

5. GDPR Compliance

Our cookie usage is fully compliant with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) because:

  • We only use essential cookies (no consent required under GDPR)
  • We provide transparent information about cookie usage
  • We do not track users or build profiles
  • We do not share cookie data with third parties
  • All cookies are secure and used only for legitimate purposes

6. Questions?

If you have questions about our cookie usage, please contact your system administrator or refer to our Privacy Policy for more information about data processing.
