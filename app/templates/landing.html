The Cost of Non-Compliance

Violating the 90/180-day rule can result in severe consequences for your business and employees

Entry Bans

Employees caught violating the 90/180-day rule face entry bans of up to 3 years from all Schengen countries, severely limiting business travel and opportunities.

Heavy Fines

Fines vary by country but can reach â‚¬5,000+ per violation. Multiple employees or repeated violations can result in cumulative penalties that significantly impact your bottom line.

Deportation & Legal Issues

Business Disruption

Unexpected travel disruptions, cancelled meetings, lost business opportunities, and damage to your company's reputation with clients and partners across Europe.
