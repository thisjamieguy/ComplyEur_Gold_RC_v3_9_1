The Challenge

UK citizens can only spend 90 days in any 180-day period across EU/Schengen countries. Manual tracking is complex, error-prone, and puts your business at risk of costly violations.

  • Rolling 180-day calculation complexity
  • Multiple employees, multiple trips
  • Immigration penalties and entry bans
  • Manual spreadsheets are unreliable

The Solution

EU Trip Tracker automatically calculates compliance for every employee, sends alerts before limits are reached, and forecasts future travel safety.

  • Real-time compliance monitoring
  • Automated alerts and forecasting
  • GDPR-compliant data storage
  • One-click Excel imports
