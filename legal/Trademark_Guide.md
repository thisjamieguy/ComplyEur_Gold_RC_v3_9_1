# Maavsi Trademark Guide (UK/EU)

1) Brand
- Word mark: "Maavsi" (primary)
- Usage: Use the mark to identify Company and products; do not use as a verb or pluralise.

2) Registration Steps (UK/EU)
- UK: File word mark "Maavsi" with UKIPO (Class suggestions: 9 software; 42 SaaS; 45 compliance consulting, as applicable).
- EU: File with EUIPO for EU‑wide protection (same classes). Consider priority from UK filing if within 6 months.
- Perform clearance search before filing (UKIPO/EUIPO databases; common law searches).

3) Proper Use
- Always include ™ while pending and ® after registration.
- Keep a consistent presentation (case, style). Avoid combining with third‑party marks.

4) Notices
- Example: Maavsi™ is a trademark of Maavsi. All rights reserved.
- After registration: Maavsi® is a registered trademark of Maavsi.

5) Third‑Party Use
- Require written permission for partners to use Maavsi marks.
- Provide brand assets and usage guidelines if licensing marks.

6) Online Protection
- Register matching domains and social handles.
- Recordations with marketplaces/app stores as needed.

7) Enforcement
- Monitor for confusingly similar names in UK/EU registers.
- Send polite but firm notices to infringers; escalate as needed.
